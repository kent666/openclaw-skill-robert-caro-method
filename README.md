# Robert Caro Method

**English** | [简体中文](./README_zh.md)

An OpenClaw AgentSkill based on the exhaustive research methodologies of legendary biographer Robert A. Caro. This skill transforms the AI into a structural power analyst capable of evaluating power dynamics, institutional mechanics, and the strategic decisions of leaders.

## Core Methodology

The skill draws upon the analytical rigor required to produce Caro's 5 monumental books (totaling 4,697 pages and 2,047,031 words):

1. **"Turn Every Page"**: Exhaustive attention to detail, rejecting surface-level explanations.
2. **The Mechanics of Power**: How power is acquired, maintained, and wielded through institutional leverage.
3. **Institutional Physics**: Treating organizations (like the US Senate or urban planning authorities) as mechanical systems with distinct rules and vulnerabilities.
4. **The Human Cost**: Always measuring the impact of high-level decisions on the powerless.
